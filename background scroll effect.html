AkmeWorld Foundation

The AkmeWorld Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to supporting the AkmeWorld ecosystem and advancing related technologies for the greater good. With a focus on sustainable development, the Foundation aims to drive positive impact by addressing key global challenges. The Foundation holds 5% of AWC (AkmeWorld Coin) and 30% of AkmeWorld DAO profit, earmarked for Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and initiatives that create long-term, sustainable value.

Purpose and Mission

The AkmeWorld Foundation is dedicated to solving critical issues impacting society and the environment. Through innovative solutions and strategic partnerships, the Foundation seeks to leverage the power of AkmeWorld’s technology to tackle pressing global problems and contribute meaningfully to the achievement of the United Nations’ SDGs.

Key Areas of Focus

Some of the primary issues the AkmeWorld Foundation aims to address include:

  • Climate change mitigation and adaptation
  • Access to clean energy and sustainable energy solutions
  • Water conservation and pollution prevention
  • Educational programs in technology and digital literacy
  • Reducing inequalities and fostering inclusive growth
  • Promoting environmental and social sustainability across industries

Funding and Support

By allocating a portion of AWC and AkmeWorld DAO profits, the Foundation ensures that resources are consistently available to fund impactful initiatives. This structure empowers the Foundation to support projects, research, and partnerships aligned with its mission, creating a cycle of continuous improvement and global benefit.
